Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 10, 2025

At Oxford Response, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Roblox game and associated services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Roblox Account Information

We collect information provided by Roblox's platform, including:

  • Roblox username and user ID
  • Avatar appearance and customization
  • Account age and creation date
  • Premium membership status

1.2 Gameplay Data

During gameplay, we collect:

  • In-game progress, ranks, and achievements
  • Department memberships and certifications
  • Play time and activity statistics
  • In-game purchases and transactions
  • Chat messages and communications (for moderation purposes)

1.3 Technical Information

We may collect technical data such as:

  • Device type and operating system
  • IP address and general location
  • Browser type and version
  • Connection and performance data

1.4 Discord and Community Platforms

If you join our Discord server or community platforms:

  • Discord username and user ID
  • Messages and interactions in our server
  • Verification and linking data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve Oxford Response gameplay experience
  • Maintain player progress, ranks, and achievements
  • Enforce community rules and moderation policies
  • Prevent cheating, exploiting, and abuse
  • Communicate updates, events, and announcements
  • Analyze gameplay patterns to improve features
  • Process in-game transactions and purchases
  • Provide customer support and assistance

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

3.1 Roblox Corporation

All data collected through Roblox is subject to Roblox's Privacy Policy and stored on their platform.

3.2 Service Providers

We may use third-party services for:

  • Analytics and performance monitoring
  • Hosting and data storage
  • Communication platforms (Discord, etc.)

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes or government requests
  • Protect our rights and property
  • Ensure safety and prevent harm
  • Investigate violations of our Terms of Service

3.4 Staff and Moderators

Our trusted staff members may access data necessary for moderation, support, and administration purposes.

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data:

  • Data is primarily stored on Roblox's secure servers
  • Access to player data is restricted to authorized staff only
  • We use encryption for sensitive communications
  • Regular security audits and updates

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services and maintain your account
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements
  • Prevent abuse and maintain security

Inactive accounts may have their data archived or deleted after extended periods of inactivity.

6. Children's Privacy

Oxford Response is available on Roblox, which is designed for users aged 13 and older. We comply with:

  • Roblox's age verification and safety measures
  • COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) requirements
  • Additional protections for younger users

Parents or guardians can contact us to request information about their child's data or request deletion.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to certain limitations)
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from communications or leave our community
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, contact us through our Discord server or Roblox group.

8. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies. See our Cookie Policy for detailed information.

The Roblox platform may also use cookies as outlined in their privacy policy.

9. Third-Party Links

Our services may contain links to third-party websites or services (Discord, YouTube, etc.). We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their privacy policies before providing any information.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country. By using Oxford Response, you consent to these transfers in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through:

  • In-game announcements
  • Discord notifications
  • Website updates

Your continued use of Oxford Response after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us through:

  • Discord: Join our server
  • Roblox Group: Oxford Response

By using Oxford Response, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and consent to the collection and use of your information as described.
